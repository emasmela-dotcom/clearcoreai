## 💬 SALES SCRIPT (Discovery Call)

### Opening (2 min)
"Thanks for taking the time! Tell me about [Company] and what brought you to ClearCoreAI?"

### Discovery (8 min)
1. "What's your biggest challenge with [area]?"
2. "How are you handling this now?"
3. "What have you tried?"
4. "If this was solved, what would that mean for your business?"

### Solution (5 min)
"Based on what you shared, here's how we'd approach this..."
- Show similar example
- Walk through features
- Explain timeline (usually 2-4 weeks)

### Pricing (3 min)
"For a project like this, we'd be looking at $[X]-$[Y]."
"Does that work with your budget?"

### Close (2 min)
"Want to move forward? I can send a proposal by EOD."
